This Week's Highlights

Smoked Beet Carpaccio

Delicate slices of smoked beetroot served with a citrus vinaigrette, fresh arugula, and toasted almond flakes. A refined, colorful appetizer to awaken the palate.

Truffle Mushroom Croquettes

Creamy croquettes made with porcini mushrooms and potatoes, infused with black truffle, and fried until perfectly golden. An indulgent bite full of umami.

Mini Chickpea & Zucchini Balls

Light, flavorful bites made with chickpeas, grated zucchini, and warm Middle Eastern spices. Served with vegan yogurt and mint dip.

Bruschetta with Vegan Ricotta & Caramelized Figs

Toasted sourdough topped with cashew-based ricotta, caramelized figs, and a touch of cracked black pepper. A perfect sweet-savory balance.

Avocado & Mango Tartare with Lime and Chili

A tropical-inspired tartare made with diced avocado and mango, marinated in lime juice and a hint of chili for a fresh and vibrant flavor.

Grilled Eggplant Rolls with Pesto & Sun-Dried Tomatoes

Thin slices of grilled eggplant rolled with homemade basil pesto and sun-dried tomatoes. A bold Mediterranean flavor in every bite.

Jackfruit BBQ Burger

Slow-cooked jackfruit in smoky barbecue sauce, served on a toasted bun with coleslaw, pickles, and vegan mayo. A mouthwatering plant-based twist on a classic.

Quinoa Stuffed Peppers

Colorful bell peppers filled with herbed quinoa, black beans, corn, and cherry tomatoes, baked until tender and served with a creamy avocado-lime sauce.

Creamy Mushroom Risotto with Truffle Oil

A rich and velvety risotto made with Arborio rice, sautéed wild mushrooms, and finished with a drizzle of white truffle oil. Comfort food, elevated.

Spiced Cauliflower Steak with Tahini Sauce

Thick-cut cauliflower steaks roasted with Middle Eastern spices, served on a bed of couscous and drizzled with lemon-tahini dressing.

Sweet Potato Gnocchi with Sage Butter

Pillowy sweet potato gnocchi sautéed in vegan butter and fresh sage, topped with crispy kale and toasted walnuts for texture and depth.

Thai Green Curry with Tofu and Vegetables

A fragrant coconut-based curry with tofu, zucchini, bell peppers, and eggplant, simmered in homemade green curry paste and served with jasmine rice.

Lavender Cheesecake

A delicate no-bake cheesecake made with soaked cashews and infused with natural lavender. Set on a crunchy almond-date crust and topped with edible flowers for a floral, elegant finish.

Chocolate Avocado Mousse

Rich, silky, and deeply chocolatey—this mousse blends ripe avocado, raw cacao, and maple syrup into a decadent yet wholesome dessert. Finished with coconut cream and cacao nibs.

Coconut Panna Cotta with Passion Fruit

A smooth panna cotta made from coconut milk, paired with a bright and tangy passion fruit coulis. Light, tropical, and dairy-free indulgence.

Baked Apple Roses with Almond Cream

Thinly sliced apples rolled into rose shapes, wrapped in vegan puff pastry, and baked to perfection. Served with a side of almond cream for a rustic yet refined dessert.

Lemon Cashew Cheesecake

Zesty and refreshing, this raw cheesecake features a lemony cashew filling over a crunchy date-almond crust. Topped with lemon zest and coconut flakes.

Chocolate Hazelnut Tart

A crisp chocolate crust filled with a creamy hazelnut ganache, made entirely without dairy. Garnished with roasted hazelnuts and a sprinkle of sea salt—pure gianduia decadence.
